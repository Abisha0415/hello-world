# Functional Decomposition 

## The spoon dropped into the gravy 

Your spoon drops in to the gravy 
    -Grab spoon 
        -Put your hand in the gravy dish 
        -Get spoon in your hands 
    -Clean spoon
        -Hold it vertically letting excess gravy drip back into the dish
  -You get your spoon out of the gravy 
        -Clean spoon 
        -Replace dirty spoon with bigger, new spoon so it does not fall in the dish again
